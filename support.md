## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How is my data stored?
RepsTrackr stores all your workout data locally on your device or cloud. We do not have access to your data.

### how do I sync my data?
RepsTrackr uses iCloud to sync your data across your devices automatically. Ensure you are signed in to iCloud with RepsTrackr enabled in your iCloud settings.

### How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual workouts or exercises directly within the app. To delete all data, simply uninstall the app. Since data is stored in your iCloud, deleting the app will also remove the local database, but iCloud data may persist until deleted via iCloud storage settings.

### Is RepsTrackr accessible?
Yes! RepsTrackr handles dynamic type, high contrast, and VoiceOver to ensure it is accessible to everyone.
