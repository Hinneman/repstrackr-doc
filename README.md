# Privacy Policy for RepsTrackr

**Effective Date**: December 1st 2025  
**Last Updated**: December 1st 2025

## Introduction

RepsTrackr ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our fitness tracking application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
RepsTrackr collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Workout Types**: Exercise names, icons, measurement types, and descriptions you create
- **Session Types**: Workout session templates with associated exercises
- **Workout Sessions**: Records of your completed workouts including:
  - Date and time of workout
  - Exercise types performed
  - Sets, reps, weights, and duration
  - Session duration

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect any personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT collect device information beyond what's necessary for app functionality
- We do NOT collect usage analytics or behavioral data
- We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies

## How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage
All your workout data is stored:
- **On iOS**: In your personal iCloud account (iCloud Drive)
- **On Android**: Locally on your device

### Data Sync
- **iOS**: Your workout data automatically syncs across your iOS devices using your personal iCloud account
- **Android**: Data remains local to your device
- **Important**: We do NOT have access to your iCloud account or any data you store

## How We Use Your Data

Your workout data is used solely to:
- Display your workout history
- Generate statistics and progress charts
- Provide workout tracking functionality
- Sync across your devices (iOS only, via your iCloud)

We do NOT:
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Sell your data
- Analyze your data on our servers (we have no servers)

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

**We do NOT share your data with any third parties.**

RepsTrackr:
- Does NOT use analytics services
- Does NOT use advertising networks
- Does NOT use third-party tracking SDKs
- Does NOT transmit data to external servers

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access
You have complete control over your workout data at all times through the app interface.

### Data Deletion
You can delete your data by:
- Deleting individual workout sessions, workout types, or session types within the app
- Uninstalling the app (note: iCloud data will persist unless manually deleted from iCloud Drive)

### Data Export
Currently, the app does not provide a data export feature. This may be added in future updates.

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device/iCloud indefinitely until you manually delete it
- Uninstalling the app does NOT automatically delete data from iCloud (iOS only)
- We do not store any data on our servers

## Children's Privacy

RepsTrackr is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

Your data security is important to us:
- All data is stored using platform-standard secure storage mechanisms
- iOS data stored in iCloud is protected by Apple's iCloud security
- No data is transmitted over the internet to our servers

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
